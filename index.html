Meet the characters

A cast built for curiosity

Each friend brings a different kind of energy into Rider's world, giving teachers and families a natural way to talk about personality, empathy, and strengths.

Hero

Rider

Rider has always wondered why he was born with a special birthmark on his arm. When it suddenly begins to change, he discovers it isn't just a mark--it holds a magical secret. As Rider follows clues with his best friends, he learns that what makes us different may be our greatest strength.

Bright Spark

Cameron

Cameron sees the world differently. With her sketchbook always close by, she uses her imagination and creativity to capture the beauty and strengths she sees in others. Her art reminds everyone that creativity can help us discover the magic that's already inside us.

Playmaker

Cooper

Cooper has a special gift for seeing the best in others. He notices strengths that people may not see in themselves and encourages everyone to believe in their own unique abilities. His caring heart and positive spirit inspire others to shine.

Spirit

Jensen

Jensen is confident, adventurous, and always willing to try something new. She faces challenges with determination, encourages others to be brave, and reminds her friends that courage isn't about never being afraid--it's about believing in yourself and taking the next step. Her fearless spirit inspires those around her to be brave in their own unique way.

Rhythm

Tripp

Tripp is full of laughter, energy, and unforgettable stories. His playful spirit has a way of bringing people together, turning ordinary moments into joyful adventures. He reminds everyone that laughter is a gift worth sharing.

Explorer

Kash

Kash has a heart that seems to know exactly when someone needs a friend. He listens with kindness, notices feelings others might miss, and always makes people feel valued and cared for. His gentle spirit teaches everyone that empathy is a powerful kind of magic--one that can brighten someone's day and make them feel like they belong.
